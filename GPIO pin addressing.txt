NOTE: In the program, location of the pin number is used 
instead of the GPIO pin number.

Red Power LED (output):
	GPIO 17 (pin 11)
Red Power Button (input):
	GPIO 27 (pin 13)
Green Play/Pause Button (input):
	GPIO 22 (pin 15)
Bottom Potentiometer (Analogue input)
	pin AIN0 (PCF 8591)
Top Potentiometer (Analogue input)
	pin AIN1 (PCF 8591)
Servo Motor feedback:
	GPIO 23 (pin 16)
Photoresistor Analogue input:
	pin AIN2 (PCF 8591)

LED Bar Graph (74HC595 module 1):
	SH_CP - GPIO 18 (pin 12)
LED Bar Graph (74HC595 module 1):
	ST_CP - GPIO 21 (pin 40)
LED Bar Graph (74HC595 module 1):
	DS - GPIO 20 (pin 38)

Ultrasound distance measure (Echo - input)
	GPIO 6 (pin 31)
Ultrasound distance measure (Trig - output)
	GPIO 5 (pin 29)

RGB LED Blue (output - PWM)
	GPIO 24 (pin 18)
RGB LED Green (output - PWM)
	GPIO 25 (pin 25)
RGB LED Red (outuput - PWM)
	GPIO 12 (pin 32)


Disco lights (Red and Green)
	GPIO 13 (pin 33)
